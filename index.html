About Me | Research Interests | Publications

Yifei ZHAO, aka Aemon Yat Fei CHIU, is a PhD student in the Department of Electronic Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) under the supervision of Professor Tan LEE. Before that, he obtained his Bachelor of Engineering in Electronic and Information Engineering with First Class Honours and a Minor in Applied Mathematics at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). His undergraduate Honours Project during his final year at PolyU was supervised by Emeritus Professor Wan-Chi SIU. He was then also a Project Assistant at the Caritas Institute of Higher Education (CIHE), assisting deep learning-related research in the Artificial Intelligence Lab in the School of Computing and Information Sciences. He joined an exchange programme at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in Academic Year 2021/22 Semester 2.
